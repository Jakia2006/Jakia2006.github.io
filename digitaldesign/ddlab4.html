Recipe for French Fries

Ingredients

  • 5 pounds of russet potatoes.
  • Vegetable or peanut oil for frying.
  • salt.

Directions

  1. peel and rinse the potatoes. Then cut them.
  2. Cover them in cold water in a large bowl. leave it for 2 or 3 hours.
  3. Heat a few inches of oil in a heavy pot to 300 drgrees F.
  4. Cook for 3 to 4 minutes.
  5. Take it out and eat it :|
